Help us translate and categorize the following questions

Reminiscens is a tablet application for stimulating face-to-face conversation around the practice of remembering and sharing our past stories. To achieve stimulation, one of features we rely on are simple life related questions that can trigger a person to remember the past. We have about 500 questions to achieve this, but they are all just in english.  Can you help us support other languages? Specifically, we would like some help in translating these questions into Italian. 

Moreover, we would also like you to help us in categorizing these questions by topic and by the period of life to which they correspond in your opinion. This will help us understand better what type of questions are best to trigger people to reminisce when they will use our application.
